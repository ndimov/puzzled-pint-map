Puzzled Pint Map

I built this map to make it easier to find Puzzled Pint events near me.

Usage

Select layers in the top right of the map.

The "Cities" layer with circular markers is based on the locations list on the homepage. Red cities are defunct, yellow are in hiatus (described recently on the webpage without a live in-person event), and green are active.

You can also view the locations of events for a particular month. Clicking on a pin gives more info. Some markers are yellow in case an exact location could not be found. Typically this means the city can't hold a live event that month.

Note

This is still under development, and suggestions or improvements are welcome! Data may be out of date or incorrect, so please check the website to confirm.
